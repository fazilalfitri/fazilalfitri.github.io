Striving for greater Indonesia

About

Fazil Alfitri is an entrepreneur, strategic advisor, and keynote speaker. He is President Director of Medco Power Indonesia, a national listed energy company in Indonesia that produces renewable energy in across different locations throughout Indonesia.

Fazil has served as president of PT. Medco Power Indonesia since 2003. His previous experience with Mobil Oil Indonesian as Development Coordinator makes him an expert in power generation business development, project finance, also oil and gas project development.

In his spare time Fazil is an active member in Berlari Untuk Berbagi, a community led organization that focuses on running marathons for charity. He has run several marathons for charity including Chicago Marathon 2016, Berlin Marathon 2015, Japan Marathon 2014.
